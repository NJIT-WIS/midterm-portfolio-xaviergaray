My Journey

Graduated Highschool

Graduated Bridgewater-Raritan Highschool after a short career in wrestling and football for two years each. I wasn't sure exactly what I wanted to do, but I enlisted in the National Guard after hearing about the opportunities they offer.

June 2019

Completed Army Initial Entry Training

Graduated as the Distinct Honor Graduate from Army Basic Combat Training and Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear (CBRN) advanced individual training. As an 18 year old, I was expected to lead adults from all walks of life and excelled at it by simply connecting with each of them and maintaining an open channel of communication.

December 2019

Enrolled at Rutgers University

Began studying at Rutgers University in pursuit of a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering for the potential to start a career in any field

January 2020

Enrolled in Rutgers Army ROTC

By this point I had started a full-time job as a laboratory assistant at LabCorp and worked on a research project studying the morphology of mice neurons. However, ROTC was an opportunity for me to truly advance my career. I was able to grow as a leader and participate in research projects sponsored by the Army Special Operations Command and MIT Lincoln Laboratory.

September 2021

MIT Lincoln Laboratory Internship

Immediately following a one month training event at Fort Knox, Kentucky, I began a mechanical engineering internship at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. Here is where I truly discovered how much I enjyoed software engineering as whatever task they threw at me, I attempted to make more efficient through software automation.

July - August 2022

Graduated Rutgers and Commissioned as an Army Officer

Graduated Summa Cum Laude alongside my peers with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and minors in Math and Military Science. In my college career, I ended up being named a scholar with the Hispanic Scholarship Fund and Louis Stokes Alliance for Minority Participation, winning Best Mechanical Engineering Project with my senior design team, and developing skills in a variety of technologies as I explored my hobbies, most notably game design. Soon after, I commissioned as a 2nd Lieutenant in the Army National Guard as a Cyber Warfare Officer.

May 2023

Software Engineer at L3 Harris

Began working as a Software Engineer at L3 Harris in the Space and Airborne Systems segment. My main duties include maintaining and developing decoders/monitors/analyzers for messages sent within F/A-18 series fighter jets.

June 2023 - Present
